If you could choose a superpower, what would it be?

The choice of superpower represents what someone is interested deep-down. It's a fun question that can lead to a lot of interesting conversations.

Similar questions: None

Tags: hypotheticals

If you were made into a classical statue, what items would you be holding?

Subjects of statues often hold objects that are symbolic and representative of their characteristics, such as a sword for a warrior or a book for a scholar. This question pushes people to think about their most defining characteristics, and what they would like to be remembered for if they were to be immortalized.

Similar questions: None

Tags: intimate, hypotheticals

Picture yourself in 10 years. What would be the worst case scenario?

This is a projection question that digs into people's fears and insecurities. You can vary this question by asking the best case scenario, which could be an alternative for understanding people's hopes and dreams.

What would be your spirit animal?

This is a fun question to ask and lighten the mood. It would also be a fun discussion to explore what 'spirit animals' mean for each person.
